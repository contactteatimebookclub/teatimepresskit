Audience and Vibe

Tea Time readers are thoughtful, creative, and deeply engaged. They love stories that feel honest, romantic, and rooted in culture.

  • Primarily Black women, 18 to 40.
  • Heavy interest in contemporary fiction, romance, fantasy, and retellings.
  • High response to journaling prompts, polls, playlists, and aesthetic visuals.

Community interests at a glance:

Black women authors
Romance and soft life
Fantasy and reimaginings
Soundtrack style playlists
Cozy digital spaces
Global community

Ways to Collaborate

Tea Time is open to thoughtful partnerships that honor story, culture, and community. Ideal collaborations include:

  • Author interviews, live Q and A sessions, and launch events.
  • Sponsor supported book club picks and reading challenges.
  • Artist and creative spotlights across visual art, music, and storytelling.
  • Playlist collaborations and mood based campaigns.

Why partners choose Tea Time:

  • Fast growing and global plus niche and deeply engaged.
  • Warm, consistent brand voice and curated visual aesthetic.
  • Real community connection, not just passive followers.
